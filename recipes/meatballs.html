Meat Balls Recipe

Home

Description

A classic meat balls recipe. It will blow your mind! Very simple to make and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Bread Crumbs

Directions

  1. Mix beef, basil and bread crumbs in bowl.
  2. Shape meat into balls
  3. Place meat balls in oven
  4. ready to eat at 450F
  5. burn your mouth
